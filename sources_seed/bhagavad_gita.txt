Bhagavad Gita (public domain translation placeholder)

Chapter 1
Dhritarashtra said: O Sanjaya, what did my sons and the sons of Pandu do, assembled at Kurukshetra, eager for battle?

Sanjaya said: Seeing the army of the Pandavas drawn up, King Duryodhana approached his teacher and spoke.

Chapter 2
Arjuna said: My mind is overwhelmed with pity, my strength is failing, and my body is quivering.

Krishna said: You grieve for those who should not be grieved for; the wise grieve neither for the living nor the dead.
